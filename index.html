Victory

2nd Prize Winners - DevXplore Hackathon 2025

Hackathons are not just competitions but also incredible learning experiences, especially for newcomers. Being the team lead of SSC_DEV_HACK, I wanted to ensure that my team members—most of whom were first-timers—understood not just the technical aspects but also the essence of collaboration, learning, and perseverance. Here's the story of how we participated, learned, grew, and emerged victorious at the DevXplore Hackathon 2k25, organized by GDG Nellore and sponsored by CAT Point.
